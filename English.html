English Department

Linguistics

The scientific study of language, including its structure, meaning, and context of use.

Literature

The study of written works, focusing on their themes, structure, and cultural significance.

Translation Theories

An exploration of various approaches to translation, emphasizing accuracy, cultural context, and meaning.

Introduction to Language

An overview of the core concepts of language, including its origin, structure, and role in society.

Semantics and Pragmatics

The study of meaning in language (semantics) and how context influences interpretation (pragmatics).

English for Specific Purposes

A course focused on teaching English skills tailored to specific fields, such as business or medicine.

Applied Linguistics

The practical application of linguistic theory to real-world problems, such as language teaching and translation.
